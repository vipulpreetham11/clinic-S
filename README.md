# Overview

ClinicOS is an AI-powered clinic management and operational intelligence platform designed for modern healthcare practices.

The platform helps clinics manage:
- appointments
- patient records
- prescriptions
- billing
- staff workflows
- follow-ups
- operational analytics
- AI-assisted healthcare workflows

through a centralized AI-native operational system.

---

# Problem

Many clinics still rely on:
- spreadsheets
- WhatsApp communication
- paper records
- fragmented systems
- manual follow-ups

This creates:
- operational inefficiencies
- patient management issues
- scheduling conflicts
- missed follow-ups
- poor data visibility

ClinicOS solves this by providing a centralized operational platform with AI-enhanced workflows and intelligent automation.
